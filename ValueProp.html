Value Proposition: Problem Statement and Benefits

Quantifying Adaptive Security for Modern Platforms

Translating bare-metal federated learning into measurable risk reduction, revenue protection, and platform trust.

Problem statement

Modern platforms face a growing class of threats that operate below the visibility of traditional OS‑centric security tools. Zero‑day exploits, firmware tampering, sideband abuse, and hardware‑level attacks increasingly drive the highest‑impact incidents—persisting beneath the OS, evading detection, and undermining platform trust. Without hardware‑rooted telemetry and bare‑metal learning, organizations remain blind to the layers where modern attackers now operate.

Defense in depth across OS and bare metal

OS-level and bare-metal defenses complement each other but have fundamentally different visibility.

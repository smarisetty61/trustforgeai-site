About TrustForgeAI™

Mission

TrustForgeAI™ builds resilient, secure, and standards-aligned infrastructure for federated AI platforms. Our mission is to bridge fragmented technology silos—from silicon to cloud—through actionable guidance, open telemetry, and platform trust.

Who We Are

What We Do

  • Publish modular schemas, visual diagrams, and telemetry pipelines
  • Enable secure onboarding, runtime assurance, and federated trust orchestration

Why It Matters

As AI workloads scale across distributed environments, platform trust and runtime assurance become critical. TrustForgeAI delivers the tools and telemetry needed to secure the future of federated AI.
